## Case management and teams

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is case management?">
    Structured incidents, investigations, project reviews, audits, and compliance cases built into the VH3 AI platform. Cases have participants, linked operational records (jobs, customers, and engineers referenced by ID), and a full activity timeline. Operational records stay in the intelligence layer; cases reference them rather than duplicating data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does case management call BigChange?">
    No. Case management is self-contained within the VH3 AI platform. No BigChange API key is required for case operations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are teams?">
    Teams are groups of users with defined goals, responsibilities, and linked operational entities that enable routine escalation, continuous background work, and agent-driven follow-through. A team can represent a region, a customer portfolio, or a function. Sentinels and automations route work to the team that owns the relevant customer or geography, so nothing sits in a generic queue.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can be a team lead?">
    Any team member can be promoted to team lead or manager by an organisation admin. Team leads can perform sensitive team actions such as accepting escalations and managing membership. Roles are enforced at the API layer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
