## Reports, notifications, and operations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What reports are available?">
    Start-of-day, midday, and close-of-business operational briefings, plus weekly account-level intelligence and ad-hoc agent-generated reports. Content is drawn from the full intelligence layer and parameterised by report type.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are notifications delivered?">
    Dashboard, email, and deep links into VH3 Connect (for example, the sentinel board or a job briefing). Automations via n8n can route notifications to Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or any connected system.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
