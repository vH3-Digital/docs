## Data, graph, and search

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is in the knowledge graph?">
    Jobs are the central node, connected to customers, engineers, job types, outcomes, worksheets, and equipment, with their full operational history. The graph captures contextual and polymorphic relationships that mirror how a field service operation actually works: the same engineer across different customers and locations, the same equipment across different sites, patterns that cross entity boundaries. That connected structure is what makes relational operational questions answerable and what enables agentic AI to reason and act across the operation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is vector search for?">
    Finding semantically similar past jobs, for example "boiler pressure fault on a commercial site," using intake descriptions and diagnostic outcomes. Results are often combined with keyword search for exact part numbers or fault codes, giving precision and meaning-based discovery in the same query.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often is data updated?">
    Job enrichment is near real-time as data lands from your FMS. Customer summaries and some aggregate data refresh on scheduled tasks. The intelligence layer lives and breathes with the ebb and flow of your business.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    Discuss export and portability with your account team. Your operational records remain in your FMS. VH3 holds enriched analytics and the operational graph. On cancellation, enriched data, summaries, and entity-resolved records are transferred to databases you control.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
