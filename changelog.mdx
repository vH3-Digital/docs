# Changelog

---

## 24 May 2026

### FAQ

Added a full Frequently Asked Questions section covering 13 topic areas and 48 questions, written to customer-facing editorial standards.

**Topics covered:**

- What is VH3 AI and how it differs from a field service management system
- Getting started, onboarding, and time to value
- API surfaces and authentication
- Security and API keys
- Data, knowledge graph, and search
- Connie and AI agents (including BYOK)
- Sentinels — how they work, severity levels, and immediate operation from day one
- Email triage and FM portal routing
- Case management and teams
- Pricing and tiers
- VH3 Connect and the developer component toolkit
- Reports and notifications
- Troubleshooting and support

Each question is rendered as a collapsible accordion. The FAQ is a top-level tab in the navigation, with all 13 sections listed individually in the sidebar.
